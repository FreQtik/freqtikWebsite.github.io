Item Workshop

Buy common items directly, craft two matching commons into improved rares, or consume recipe ingredients to forge powerful epic and legendary items. Equipped items count as busy, so unequip them before crafting.

Crafting basics: Common pairs upgrade into specialized rare items. Recipes consume the listed ingredients and create one finished item. This gives you reliable long-term scaling instead of waiting only for boss drops.
