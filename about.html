Independent producer and developer

Music, tools and
interactive ideas.

FreQtik is the independent work of a producer and developer creating audio software, practical producer resources, games and small interactive projects.

Most projects begin with something I want to solve, understand or make easier to use. Some become tools for music production; others become games, training projects or experiments. They are different kinds of work, but they all come from the same hands-on curiosity.
