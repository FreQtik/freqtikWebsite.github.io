What it does

One job: make your DAW audible outside the DAW.

Built for Windows producers who can hear their DAW locally, but their call, screen share or recording software receives silence.

1

Stream your master

Use it for Discord calls, OBS recordings, remote feedback sessions and screen sharing when normal desktop capture misses ASIO audio.

2

Check other outputs fast

Route a copy to another connected device, monitor, HDMI output, USB interface or small speaker without changing your DAW audio driver.

3

Stay out of the mix

It is not EQ, saturation, compression, loudness or mastering. It is a routing utility for a specific producer workflow problem.

FAQ

Quick answers.

Does it only work in Cubase?

No. It is a Windows VST3 utility. It was first tested in Cubase, but the concept is DAW-agnostic: insert it on the master of any Windows VST3 host.

Can I use it to test speakers or other devices?

Yes. It can mirror your DAW master to another connected Windows output, which makes quick device checks easier.

Is it a mastering plugin?

No. It is not designed to change the sound. It is a utility for routing the DAW master into Windows audio workflows.

Should I use it on every track?

No. Use one active instance, normally as the last insert on the master or stereo output.
